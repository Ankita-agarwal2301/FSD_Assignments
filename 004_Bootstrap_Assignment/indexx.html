ABOUT

Starting out as Mayuri in 1997, we inaugrated our first restaurant in the Las Colinas area providing fresh Indian cuisine to the residents of Dallas. Mayuri was recognized as one of the market leaders in Indian Cuisine serving Dallas metropolitan area for many years. Our delicious food and catering services were widely appreciated by guest and media alike. In 2010, we moved to a much larger new location at the intersection of MacArthur Blvd and I-635. At this location we had a large seating area and an attached banquet hall for conducting small to medium sized events. 7 years later and at our brand new location, We are JHA Kitchen. With over 18,700 sqft of space, JHA Kitchen is the largest Indian Restaurant in North America. Serving a buffet of 101 items, multiple event halls, a specialty indian sweet shop, a fine dining bar and lounge with a second floor patio, our new facility is like none other.
